# Airbnb-Smart-Price-Prediction

## Predict Prices for Airbnb listings and provide business insights


- A major issue in planning a holiday trip to other city/country is finding a cheap accommodation online and save prices on reservation
- Airbnb is a peer-to-peer online marketplace for arranging homestay solutions which allows users to rent and book residential properties
- This project provides insights to users on what are the principal factors majorly influences the price of listings in Airbnb and how to effectively use the insights to their own situation
    - I.	To provide insights to customers who wishes to rent listings from Airbnb on how to choose best listing for cheap price and save money
    - II.	To provide insights to renters who post their place on Airbnb for rent on how much renting price to fix for their listings and yearn more profits.
